Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a versatile Italian staple made from wheat and water, often shaped into various forms like spaghetti, penne, or fusilli. It is typically boiled until al dente and can be paired with a wide range of sauces, from simple marinara to rich Alfredo. Pasta is enjoyed globally for its adaptability and comforting nature.

Ingredients

  • Pasta-Any type, such as spaghetti, penne, or fusilli.
  • Olive oil-For cooking and adding flavor.
  • Garlic-For a simple, aromatic touch.
  • Salt-For seasoning the pasta water and the dish.
  • Parmesan cheese-For topping and adding a savory finish.

Steps

  1. Boil water:Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook pasta:Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Prepare garlic oil:While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Combine:Drain the cooked pasta and toss it in the pan with the garlic oil, mixing well.
  5. Serve:Plate the pasta and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
