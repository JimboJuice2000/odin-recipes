Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular dish consisting of a round, flat crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is baked until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Pizza can be customized with an array of toppings, making it a versatile and beloved meal worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough-Home-made or store bought
  • Tomato sauce-Simple marinara or pizza sauce
  • Cheese-Mozzarella is the most common choice.
  • Toppings-To add on top of your pizza
  • Olive oil-For brushing the dough.

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough:Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  2. Add the sauce:Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  3. Add cheese:Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  4. Bake:Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  5. Serve:Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, slice, and serve hot.
